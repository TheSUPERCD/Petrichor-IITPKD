About Us

Indian Institute of Technology Palakkad belongs to the new generation of IITs. It is an autonomous public institution established in the year 2015. Within a short time span, our institute started offering BTech, MS, MTech, MSc and Phd programs.As of now, we are located in two campuses, viz, the Ahalia integrated campus(Temporary Campus) and the Nila Campus(Permanent Campus) at Kanjikode.The Nila campus is situated at the foot of the Western Ghats, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

IIT Palakkad is proud to introduce its Techno-Cultural fest, Petrichor. Petrichor founded in the year 2018, aims to break the loop of monotony that everyday lie has to offer and celebrate the new dawns that await us. Petrichor provides an ideal platform for the students, faculty, participants and sponsers by hosting a plethora of Technical and Cultural events where participants can exhibit their technical and Cultural proficiency.From 6 cultural events and 5 technical events in the first year to 17 cultural events and 9 technical events last year, Petrichor is evolving every year!

Campus Ambassador Incentives

For all Campus Ambassadors:

  • Cash prize for top 5 Campus Ambassadors.
  • Top 5 active Campus Ambassadors will be provided with free workshop and guest-lecture passes on the day of fest.
  • Top 30 active Campus Ambassadors will be awarded with a certificate of merit.
  • Special Discount on Pro-show entry passes will be provided to top 10 active Campus Ambassadors.
  • Special discount on Petrichor goodies will be given to top 10 active Campus Ambassadors.

If Campus Ambassador has 10+ participants:

  • Special discount on workshop or guest-lecture passes on the day of fest.
  • Free Pro-show tickets.
  • Special discount on Petrichor goodies.

If Campus Ambassador has 30+ participants:

  • Free workshop or guest-lecture passes on the day of fest.
  • Free accomodation on the day of fest.
  • Free Pro-show tickets.
  • Special discount on Petrichor goodies.

Note:

  • The performance of Campus Ambassadors will be judged on the basis of points they earn and the work they do.
  • Participants should not be from online events and each participant should be a different person.
