About Us

Indian Institute of Technology Palakkad is an autonomous public institution established in 2015 and within a short span of time our institute is already offering BTech, MS, MTech, MSc and PhD programmes with nearly 80 faculty, 700 students and 60 administrative & technical staff from all over the country. As of now, we are located at two campuses, viz, the Ahalia integrated campus (Temporary Campus) and the Nila Campus (Permanent Campus) at Kanjikode which is fast coming up on a picturesque 500-acre plot with majestic Western Ghats visible on one side.


Proud to present to you, the annual techno-cultural fest of IIT Palakkad, Petrichor. Petrichor, with its foundation in the year 2018, is not just a fest for us but a journey to define ourselves.With a plethora of events to showcase your technical expertise or put on a show of cultural extravaganza during a period of two days, Petrichor provides the perfect platform for students, faculty, participants from all around and sponsors to come together and cherish technology and science and our unique culture. From 16 cultural events and 5 technical events in its first year to 17 cultural and 9 technical events last year, Petrichor promises to be bigger and better every coming year!
