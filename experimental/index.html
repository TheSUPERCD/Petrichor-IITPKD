ABOUT

The early years of this century saw some aspiring students garner around in the hope to behold the seeds of ideas that sprouted in their minds take life before them. This resulted in one of south India's biggest scholastic endeavors - Tathva, the techno-management fest of NIT Calicut. Since it's inception in 2000, this annual symposium has known only growth, and has flourished into one of the few promising enterprises the nation has witnessed.

Pushing the barriers year after year, Tathva aims at providing a plethora of platforms to the intrigued technical heads that arrive at this fiesta with an array of events and workshops, to sharpen their skills and widen their knowledge quotient. With the engine up and roaring, Tathva'19 is all set to embark on a new expedition, proffering opportunities and paving trails to greater comprehension. From being in awe of the top-notch robotic & automobile display and the adrenaline rush while racing against time and competing with some of the tech-wired geniuses out there, to the ecstatic ambience of the pronites, Tathva has it all covered for it's audience. With Adizya displaying an impressive arena for the art lovers and designing heads, we call out to all the right and left brains alike. Come, embark on this journey with us, and let's drift away from normalcy and let the magic seep into our souls.

Wheels

Float like a Cadillac, Sting like a Beemer. Drive not to save time, drive to have a good time. Witness one of the biggest gathering of automobile enthusiasts from south India display a breathtaking range of customized exotic rides in WHEELS!

Lectures

Delve into the enigmas of the science era by being a part of the Tathva lectures. Lend your ears to the talks that enrapture the tech-savvy souls. This is where proficiency and deliverance entwines and brings theories to startle your boggling neurons. Experience the chants of the tech-gurus.

Exhibitions

The mind boggling puzzles laid out by the Club Mathematica, innovative projects put forth by the different branches and the assortment of robots, cars and planes presented by our zealous technical clubs are just tip of the iceberg of the true spirit embodied within the Tathva. Apart from being a platform to exhibit the essence of each club, the Exhibitions personify the year around work and enthusiasm of our young minds.

Proshows

Shimmering lights, reverberating rhythms, they add to the glee and glory of the nights of the technical extravaganza,Tathva. Years down, the stage has been set for the renowned voices of the industry. Being the nights that enthrall the crowd, Proshows steal the limelight throughout the days. It is a show to behold, a glance to get.

Interface

Go to website - interface.tathva.org

Over the past few years Interface has changed the landscape for young entrepreneurs in South India by providing a platform to implement their ingenious ideas. Interface begins with a mentoring weekend ahead of the Tathva Interface in which the selected teams interact with their mentors. The Tathva Interface brings in the events including workshops, pitching, panel discussions and talks from some of the most successful entrepreneurs in India. Interface continues to be the face of student ingenuity and dedication and its certain that its bright flame is not going to burn up any time soon.

Blitzkrieg

Blitzkrieg, present by Tathva'18, is a testament of how versatile the gaming junkies of Kerala are. Taking things to the next level, it was the ultimate paradise for the gaming community. From football to shooter games, the adrenaline could be felt coursing through the air. It is definitely one for the books!

Samasya

Conducted by Enquire Quiz club, Samasya School Quiz is an attempt to reach out to schools, presenting them a unique quizzing session where young quizzers can test their mettle against each other. With prelims conducted across different venues in the state and huge participation every year it is definitely Kerala's biggest quizzing event. Selected teams then compete in the most fiery battle of brains in the state finals. Being one of the most enthralling events of Tathva, Samasya proves as a crowd puller every year.

Aavishkar

The social initiative of Tathva was established to extend a compassionate helping hand to the less fortunate. By focusing on alleviating the sufferings of the weaker sessions of the society, to uplift and empower them, AAVISHKAAR became a grand success in the past few years.
Well-planned and comprehensive programmes in healthcare and education are conducted every year by this brainchild of Tathva.

Adizya

Accompanied by a grand website launch, the works of craft and colors on the canvas were successful in attracting the crowd Adizya well deserved. Encouraging creative minds to let their imagination flow in a number of open participation events, educating them through multiple lectures by men of repute and through the exhibition itself it accomplished it's vision and left an undeniably beautiful mark in our memories.

Informals

The informals, presented by tathva 18' is the embodiment of the true nature of the tech fest Tathva. These series of various events held at random locations on the Campus held the wonder and bewilderment of the audience. The folks will return, with no less exuberance hoping for the next informals!

Workshops

From analyzing the intricacies of the commercial world to delving into the fast paced changes in AI, our pragmatic workshops can transform seemingly morbid ideas to exciting industrial avenues. Come and partake in this fiasco of active knowledge and discover more.
